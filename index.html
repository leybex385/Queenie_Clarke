About

Queenie Clarke is an entrepreneur and investor with a background spanning international business, medical technology, and cross-border capital strategy. Raised in Singapore and later based in Los Angeles and New York, she brings a global perspective shaped by education at the National University of Singapore and hands-on experience in operating businesses across healthcare, beauty, and medical device distribution. Her work centers on building and scaling platforms rather than isolated ventures, combining disciplined investment thinking, operational execution, and long-term value creation across global markets.
